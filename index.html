A Guide For The Beginners

The biggest superhero franchise had it’s launch in 2008 with the release of Iron man. The franchise has since then grown bigger and with over 23 movies and 2 series, makes it hard to keep a track of all characters in the franchise. It gets difficult to remember who loves whom, who hates whom, who killed whom, and who is back from the dead, the past, or the future. There are many in-depth guides to the MCU, but sometimes they have many details which makes it easy to get lost, and if you struggle to remember characters who have multiple names makes it even harder to keep a track of what’s going on.

Therefore I welcome you to this website that’ll help you keep a track of what’s happening in the MCU. We’ll post the latest news about release dates of movies, tv-series, characters information, and recommended youtube channels that talk about the MCU.

How To Watch

As I mentioned before, the MCU has more than 23 movies and 2 series, there are different ways to watch them. The movies take place over several years and are connected to each other, there are 2 different ways to watch them. One way to watch them is the years they take place in (chronological order) or in the years they were released (release date order) in theaters. You can get spoiled if you watch them in chronological order. But if you want to watch the movies in chronological order, you might get minor spoilers.
